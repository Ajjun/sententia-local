 Q   What is Sententia?
Sententia is a cloud-based, individualized, formal writing assistive tool for students and professionals. Sententia helps in improving the writing in general and use of formal writing style in particular, over a period of time. Sententia achieves this goal by describing the wrong or inappropriate usage with correct-usage examples and explaining the underlying formal writing style or grammar rules.


 Q  How is it different from other tools in the market?
Sententia, in addition to grammar correctness, also focuses on adherence to formal writing style. The strength of Sententia is the individualization - thorough analysis of the writing style of the individual, common mistake the individual makes and the growth the individual undergo in his/her writing, over a period of time. If your goal is to improve your formal writing over a period of time, then Sententia is your solution. However, If you just want to fix a single document - it may not be the right solution.

 Q  How are the issues in my writing shown?
  • In Sententia, there are 4 different categories of document analysis output.
  • Suggestions: This category include those sentences does a negligible violation of formal writing rule(s). These are shown in green and it is OK to overlook.
  • Guidelines: Guidelines are those that violate any formal writing rule(s), though they may be grammatically correct. These are shown in blue and it is advised not to overlook.
  • Bugs: These are serious violation of either formal writing rule or the English Grammar. These are shown in Red and should not be overlooked.
  • Analysis charts: These charts categorize the issues into different types and give the reader an idea about the type and count of violations he/she is doing.
